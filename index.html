About Me

I'm Nate, a Full Stack Developer

I started my career in sales after graduating from Indiana University-Bloomington with a BS in Healthcare Management and Policy. After having success at All Covered the IT Services division of Konica Minolta, I joined a 4 person startup called Stokkup Software as the team's first Salesman.

At Stokkup, I learned what it takes to start and grow a company from the ground up and how to deal with constant adversity. Monitoring my clients' usage of the Stokkup platform became a big part of my job, and lead me to learn to run my own MySQL queries. This piqued my interest in software development.

I then attended General Assembly for the Software Engineering Immersive Program. This propelled me to where I am today, assistant teaching the program with my former professors, helping people just like myself learn to code and make a major step in their career!
